Blender Salsa

This recipe was submitted by Susan Paz

Description

This recipe is for a spicy blended salsa.

This is a fresh salsa using ingredients easily found in Ecuador.

Ingredients

  • 5 Roma Tomatoes
  • 1 Green Onion
  • 1/2 White Onion
  • 2 Jalapenos
  • 1/2 Bunch of Cilantro
  • Juice of 4 Small Limes
  • 1 Tablespoon of Salt
  • 1 Small Spoonful of Minced Garlic

Steps

  1. Blend ingredients.
  2. Add additional ingredients to taste.
