Crawfish Pie

This recipe was submitted by Chris Paz Ryc

Description

This recipe is for mini crawfish pies.

The recipe is easily frozen if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Chopped Onion
  • 1 Cup Celery (two stalks)
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Stick Butter
  • 1 Heaping Tablespoon Flour
  • 1 Can Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 Can Evaporated Milk or 1 Cup Regular Milk
  • 2 lbs Peeled Crawfish Tails
  • 2 or 3 Tablespoons Minced Green Onion
  • 2 or 3 Tablespoons Minced Parsley
  • 1/4 Cup White Wine
  • To Taste:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Worcestershire Sauce
    • A Few Drops of Liquid Crab Boil
  • Mini Pie Crusts

Steps

  1. Saute onions, celery, and garlic in butter.
  2. Add flour and mix well. It's not necessary to brown the ingredients.
  3. Add the soup and milk and cook for ten minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. Add crawfish, green onions, parsley, and wine and cook until tender.
  5. Season to taste.
  6. Pour mixture into uncooked pie shells. If you'd like to freeze them at this point you may.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees until the crust is golden brown.
