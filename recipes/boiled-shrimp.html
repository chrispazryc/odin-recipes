Boiled Shrimp

This recipe was submitted by Susan Paz

Description

This recipe is for a simple boiled shrimp that is seasoned but otherwise plain.

To help reduce undesired smells, set out a bowl of vinegar while cooking.

Note: If using peeled shrimp, only boil for one minute.

Ingredients

  • Bag of Shrimp Boil (seasoning)
  • Lemon
  • Onion
  • Bay Leaf
  • Salt
  • Shrimp

Steps

  1. Boil enough water to cover the shrimp.
  2. Add in the shrimp boil bag, a lemon, a quartered onion, a bay leaf or two, and two tablespoons of salt.
  3. Bring to a rolling boil.
  4. Add shrimp.
  5. Boil for 3 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and cover for 20 minutes.
  7. Peel and enjoy!
